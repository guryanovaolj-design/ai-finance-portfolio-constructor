2 · Brand Positioning

The empty quadrant on the Serbian brokerage map is transparency × accessibility. Tesla Capital owns it by combining the heritage cue (25+ years) with the digital cue (the simplest, most documented, most affordable way to start investing in Serbia).

"Tesla Capital — 25 godina iskustva, transparentne provizije, prvi račun za 48 sati. Berza za sve — od prvog dinara do prvog miliona."

How Tesla Capital should differ from each competitor

Competitor leads withTesla counters with
Ilirika — "30+ years, premium asset management, HNWI minimum"Same heritage signal, but without the gate — Tesla welcomes investors from the first dinar, with the same depth on the HNWI/corporate end.
Momentum — "Iskusno. Predano. Transparentno." with the best content brandTesla's content is actionable, not editorial — every article ends with a calculator, a quiz, or a one-click contact. Knowledge that converts.
Prudence — "Sigurno sklonište od inflacije" + Bloomberg + IBKR + TarifnikTesla matches the transparency (Tarifnik) and adds what Prudence missed: published model-portfolio performance, testimonials, mobile portal, and a faster onboarding promise.

The four pillars of the positioning

01

Transparentnost

Public Tarifnik, public model-portfolio NAV, named team, plain-language risk disclosures.

02

Dostupnost

"Prvi račun za 48 sati", three-door homepage (Početnik / Aktivan / Korporacija–HNWI), mobile portal.

03

Iskustvo

25 godina na tržištu, licencirani brokeri sa CFA/MBA, supervizija KHoV, partnerstva sa kastodi bankama.

04

Edukacija

Tesla Akademija sa besplatnim kursevima, kalkulatorima, kvizovima i mesečnim biltenom.

Modernizing the perception of a traditional brokerage

  1. Lead with people, not towers. Replace stock skyline imagery with original photography of named bankers, the actual Belgrade office, and real clients (with permission).
  2. Custom illustration system for blog, social, and calculators — none of the three competitors owns illustration, so it becomes Tesla's instant visual signature.
  3. Plain-language tone: short sentences, no jargon without an inline definition, written so a first-year economics student understands without losing the depth a CFO needs.
  4. Show the platform. Embed live product screenshots, micro-demos, and a public sandbox where prospects can place a paper-trade without registering.

Communicating trust, expertise, and accessibility

  • Trust: regulator badge (Komisija za hartije od vrednosti) + KHoV registration number + named portfolio managers with credentials in the homepage trust bar.
  • Expertise: founder's monthly column ("Pismo investitorima") + CFA-led research notes + annual outlook PDF.
  • Accessibility: "Otvori račun za 48h" as a permanent top-bar CTA, three-door homepage segmentation, free Akademija content, and a mobile-first client portal.

3 · Target Audience Strategy

Five distinct personas, each with its own door, message, and service stack. The homepage routes by intent; nothing is one-size-fits-all.

Segment A

Retail investors / beginners

25–45, first-time investors, often Belgrade/Novi Sad professionals or freelancers, EUR 500–10,000 ticket.

Pain points

  • "Ne znam odakle da počnem"; afraid of jargon and of losing money
  • Bank deposits no longer outrun inflation; FX savings feel passive
  • Tax on capital gains in Serbia is confusing

Motivators

  • Doing something concrete with savings rather than letting them stagnate
  • Learning a real skill (financial literacy as personal growth)
  • Belonging to a community of investors

Messaging that resonates

  • "Počnite od 100 evra. Vodimo vas korak po korak."
  • "Berza objašnjena ljudski."
  • "Ne morate da budete ekspert da biste počeli — moramo mi."

Services to emphasize

  • Brokerske usluge sa fokusom na ETF-ove (low-cost, diversifikovano)
  • Indeksirani dugoročni plan / DCA
  • Tesla Akademija + bilten
Segment B

Experienced retail / active traders

30–55, već trguju, koriste IBKR/eToro/lokalne brokere, ticket EUR 10k–100k.

Pain points

  • Visoke provizije na lokalnim brokerima
  • Slabe platforme i loš mobilni UX
  • Nedostupna istraživanja na srpskom jeziku

Motivators

  • Brzina izvršenja, niska provizija, kvalitetna istraživanja
  • Pristup što većem broju berzi (US, EU, EM)
  • Profesionalna podrška kada zatreba (margin, derivati)

Messaging

  • "Pristup za 150+ berzi. Provizija koju vidite unapred."
  • "Nedeljni Tesla Insight: 3 ideje, 3 grafikona, 3 zaključka."
  • "Vaš broker — kada zatreba čovek, a ne čekanje."

Services to emphasize

  • Brokerske usluge sa transparentnim Tarifnikom + WEB platforma
  • IBKR / globalni pristup
  • Istraživanja i mesečni outlook PDF
Segment C

HNWI / private wealth

40–65, EUR 100k–5M imovine za investiranje, preduzetnici i top menadžeri.

Pain points

  • Bankarska privatna usluga je tanka, šablonska, sa visokim naknadama
  • Nedostatak personalizovane strategije, samo "predlozi"
  • Strah od poverenja sredstava bez jasnog izveštavanja

Motivators

  • Očuvanje i sukcesivni prenos imovine (legacy)
  • Diskrecija, dostupnost konkretne osobe, kvartalni izveštaji
  • Pristup investicionim temama (zelene tehnologije, AI, alternativna ulaganja)

Messaging

  • "Tesla Capital Private — jedan menadžer, jedan plan, jedan izveštaj kvartalno."
  • "Strategije za očuvanje i uvećanje kapitala kroz generacije."
  • "Diskretno, mereno, sa potpisom."

Services to emphasize

  • Diskrecioni portfolio menadžment (više modela rizika)
  • Investiciono savetovanje + estate / sukcesija (uz pravne partnere)
  • Tematska ulaganja, alternativna ulaganja, strane berze
Segment D

Korporativni klijenti / SMB treasury

CFO/finansijski direktori srpskih SME i mid-cap kompanija, viškovi gotovine, potreba za hartijama.

Pain points

  • Viškovi gotovine na depozitima donose negativan realni prinos
  • Nedovoljno znanja o korporativnoj agenturi i izdanjima obveznica
  • Strah od kompleksnosti pravne/regulatorne strane

Motivators

  • Optimizacija prinosa na kratkoročnu i srednjoročnu likvidnost
  • Pristup tržištu kapitala kao alternativi banci za financiranje
  • Transparentno izveštavanje za upravu i bord

Messaging

  • "Vaš trezor zaslužuje plan, ne depozit."
  • "Od izdanja korporativne obveznice do likvidnosnog portfolija — pod jednim krovom."
  • "Stručna usluga za vaš CFO tim."

Services to emphasize

  • Korporativna agentura i izdanja obveznica
  • Finansijsko-pravni konsalting
  • Trezorske strategije (laddering, repo, kratkoročne hartije)
Segment E

Mlađi investitori (25–40) — digital-native

Tech sektor, kreativna profesija, freelancers, primaju u EUR/USD, troše u RSD, već razmišljaju o ETF-ovima i kripto.

Pain points

  • Niko im ne objašnjava poreze, devizne tokove, otvaranje računa "ljudski"
  • Lokalni brokeri deluju zastarelo i nedostupno
  • Strah od propuštenih prilika (FOMO) vs. strah od greške

Motivators

  • Automatizacija (DCA, mesečno povlačenje sa kartice)
  • Edukacija u kratkom, vizuelnom formatu (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube)
  • Bilo ko sa kim mogu da razgovaraju — ne pisani formular

Messaging

  • "Berza, ali bez čekanja od dva sata u banci."
  • "Vaš prvi račun. Vaš prvi dividendni dan."
  • "Učite na Tesla Akademiji. Investirajte iz aplikacije."

Services to emphasize

  • Otvaranje računa "24 sata", mobile-first portal
  • SIP / DCA proizvod sa malim minimalcem
  • Akademija + bilten + sociali kao stalni edukativni kanal

4 · Website & UX Recommendations

Ten website improvements distilled from competitor strengths and gaps. Sequence is roughly day-30 (1–4), day-60 (5–7), day-90 (8–10).

The ten improvements

  1. Three-door homepage segmentation. Below the hero, three large clickable doors: Početnik · Aktivan investitor · Korporacija / HNWI. Each routes to its own landing page with a tailored CTA. None of the three competitors does this.
  2. Single-message hero with a quote-led headline + 3-logo trust bar. Prudence proved the format works. Tesla's trust bar: Komisija za hartije od vrednosti · 25 godina iskustva · Kastodi banka partner.
  3. Always-visible "Otvori račun za 48h" primary nav button and sticky bottom-bar CTA on mobile.
  4. Public Tarifnik page — match-or-beat Prudence and add a comparator vs. depozit / vs. domaća banka / vs. IBKR direktno.
  5. Named team page with photos, CFA/FRM/MBA credentials, LinkedIn links, and a one-line "specijalizacija" per banker.
  6. Tesla Akademija hub with 15 lessons, a glossary, three quizzes, and a "Vodič za investitore" pillar. Akademija links from every blog article.
  7. Calculator suite — at minimum: složena kamata, inflacija (RSD/EUR), porez na kapitalnu dobit, brokerske provizije, FX trošak. Each ends with "Razgovarajte sa savetnikom".
  8. Model-portfolio dashboard — monthly-updated NAV of a representative portfolio per risk tier, with disclaimers. Category-first.
  9. Mobile-first client portal (web before native app) — login, portfolio view, statements, deposit instructions, push e-mail alerts.
  10. English-language toggle on the header — diaspora and Belgrade-domiciled foreign HNWIs.

Homepage recommendations (sketch)

  1. Hero: short quote-led headline + trust bar (3 logos) + "Otvori račun" + "Saznajte više" secondary.
  2. Three doors (Početnik / Aktivan / Korporacija–HNWI).
  3. Three "Prudence-style" credibility numbers (klijenti / AUM / godina iskustva) — one canonical pair, no duplicates.
  4. Six service tiles with one-line each + "Detaljnije".
  5. Tesla Akademija block — three featured lessons + email capture.
  6. Model-portfolio mini-widget (latest monthly NAV per tier).
  7. Tesla Insight — featured market commentary card + LinkedIn link.
  8. Testimonial / logo strip — 6 named clients + 8–10 corporate logos.
  9. "Pitajte savetnika" form — short, low-friction (ime, e-mail, pitanje).
  10. Footer with regulator badge, KHoV registration number, both office addresses, social links.

  • "O nama" page with named team, photographed at the office, with credentials and short bios.
  • "U medijima" press-mentions wall (Bloomberg Adria, Forbes Srbija, Bizlife, Politika, Nova ekonomija, NIN).
  • "Klijenti" page — 6 named retail testimonials + corporate logo wall.
  • "Sigurnost klijentskih sredstava" page — explains custody, KHoV supervision, kompenzacioni fond.

Suggested navigation

Primary nav: O nama · Usluge · Tarifnik · Otvori račun · Akademija · Insight · Kontakt with sub-menus only under O nama, Usluge, Akademija, Insight.

Making investing feel easier and less intimidating

  • Plain-language UI copy ("Koliko želite da uložite?" instead of "Inicijalni ulog").
  • Inline glossary tooltips on technical terms (ETF, dividenda, P/E).
  • Risk-tolerance quiz before the contact form — gamifies the first step.
  • "Šta očekujete u prvih 30 dana" timeline on the "Otvori račun" page.
  • Soft chat / WhatsApp / Viber widget — answers from a real broker, not a bot.

8 · Brand Modernization Recommendations

The category looks the same from 10 ft — dark blue + red accent + city stock photos + numbered tiles. Tesla can break that pattern with three moves: original photography, an owned illustration system, and editorial typography.

Making Tesla Capital feel modern and approachable

  • Replace every stock skyline / handshake / globe photo with original photography of named bankers, the office, and (with permission) clients.
  • Switch from generic geometric vectors to an owned illustration system — friendly, plain-language, used across blog, social, calculators, and the Akademija.
  • One coherent typography pair: a quality editorial serif for headlines + a Serbian-Cyrillic-and-Latin sans for UI (e.g. Inter or IBM Plex Sans).
  • Restrained motion (8–12 small animations site-wide). Less is more — none of the competitors uses motion, so even a modest investment is differentiating.
  • Dark mode + AA accessibility. Cheap, visible, signals "we care".

Tone of voice

  • Razumljivo, ali ozbiljno. Kratak, jasan, srpski jezik, bez praznog korporativnog tona.
  • Mi vs. ti / Vi. Početnicima — "ti", direktno, prijateljski. HNWI i korporativnim — "Vi", uz poštovanje protokola.
  • Nikada strah. Ne prodajemo kroz strah od inflacije ili strah od propuštanja — već kroz mogućnost i kontrolu.
  • Uvek primer. Svaka tvrdnja sa konkretnim primerom, brojem, ili grafikonom.
  • Bez praznog hvalisanja. Tvrdnje koje se mogu proveriti — godina, broj klijenata, AUM, licence — uz link na izvor.
